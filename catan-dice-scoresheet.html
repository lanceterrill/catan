Objective
Score the most points after 15 rounds. Each round, roll 6 dice up to 3 times (Yahtzee-style), then spend the resources you've rolled to build roads, settlements, cities, and knights on your personal map sheet.
Rolling the Dice
Each die face shows one of 6 resources: 🧱 Brick · 🪵 Lumber · 🌾 Grain · 🐑 Wool · ⛏ Ore · 💰 Gold
Roll 1: Roll all 6 dice. Set any keepers aside.
Roll 2: Re-roll the rest. You may also pick back up dice you set aside earlier.
Roll 3: Final roll. After this, your resources are locked (unless you use a Resource Joker or Gold).
Building Costs
🛤 Road
1 Brick + 1 Lumber
1 pt each · max 15 · must build in sequence
🏘 Settlement
1 Lumber + 1 Brick + 1 Grain + 1 Wool
1 pt each · max 5 · must be adjacent to a built road · build in point order
🏙 City
3 Ore + 2 Grain
2 pts each · max 4 · must upgrade an existing settlement
⚔ Knight
1 Ore + 1 Grain + 1 Wool
0 pts individually · max 7 · unlocks Resource Joker & Largest Army
Special Bonuses
🛣 Longest Road (+2 pts): First player to build all 15 roads claims it. If the other player catches up, they take it — the previous holder loses the 2 pts.
⚔ Largest Army (+2 pts): First player to build 3 knights claims it. Again, transferable if the other player builds more knights.
💰 Gold: Each gold die face can substitute for any one resource. Gold does not directly score points — it just flexes into whatever you need.
Knights & Resource Jokers
Each knight you build earns you a one-time Resource Joker: after your third roll, you may turn one die to show any resource face. Mark the knight as used when you do. Build 3 knights to claim Largest Army (+2 pts).
Scoring Your Round
After building, total up all points earned this turn and write it in the next open round box. If you build multiple things, add them together into one number. If you build nothing, write an X — it costs you 2 points at the end.
Winning
After all 15 rounds, add up your round scores, subtract 2 pts for each X, and add any active Longest Road / Largest Army bonuses. Highest total wins.
💡 Tip: Roads unlock settlements, settlements unlock cities. Don't neglect your road chain — it's the backbone of your whole score.
